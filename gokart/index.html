Personal Project

Go-Kart Rebuild and Mechanical Integration

Full drivetrain, steering, braking, and suspension replacement and alignment — restoring a high-performance go-kart to a consistent ~45 mph.

Overview

This go-kart came in with a list of drivetrain, braking, and steering problems that made it unsafe to run at speed. I rebuilt it end to end: drivetrain, steering, brakes, and suspension, replacing worn components and re-aligning the system so performance held up consistently rather than degrading after a few runs.

Highlights

  • Rebuilt a high-performance go-kart (~45 mph) through full drivetrain, steering, braking, and suspension replacement and alignment.
  • Diagnosed and resolved 3+ drivetrain, braking, and steering failures through iterative mechanical troubleshooting, restoring consistent performance at ~45 mph.
